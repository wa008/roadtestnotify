# How to book a road test fast in Ontario?

You get three chances to reschedule your road test within a six-month window. This means you should always book the earliest available date right away.

Once you have your initial appointment, you can start checking the booking schedule and refreshing the page frequently to find an earlier, more convenient slot. The good news is the system won't cancel your existing appointment until you successfully find and book a new one!
