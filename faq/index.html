Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service

Q1:How I can use the service?
After you subscribe current service, you will get SMS notification like example in main page as soon as a new recent road test slot released, you don't have to refresh the drivetest website again and again. What you need to do is book road test since you receive the SMS notification.
Q2:How to choose my test types and locations?
You can choose that in payment page after choosing the plan.
Q3:When I can receive the notification?
You can receive notification no more than 20 minutes after a new recent slot release generally.
