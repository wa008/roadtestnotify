Ontario Road Test Alerts FAQ

Common questions about our Ontario G and G2 alert service

Q1:How does the service work?
After you subscribe, we monitor the Ontario DriveTest booking system for your selected test type and test centre. When an earlier road test slot appears, we send you an SMS notification like the example on the home page so you can book it right away.
Q2:How do I choose my test type and test centre?
You choose your preferred G or G2 test and your preferred Ontario test centre on the subscribe page when you select a plan.
Q3:How quickly will I receive a notification?
In most cases, you will receive an SMS notification within about 20 minutes after a new road test slot is released.
Q4:Will I receive a confirmation after I subscribe?
Yes. After your payment is completed, we send a confirmation SMS to the mobile number you entered when subscribing. If you do not receive it within 10 minutes, please contact us and we will help confirm your subscription.
Q5:Can I reply directly to the SMS notification?
Please do not reply directly to our SMS notifications — the number is used for outbound alerts only and replies are not monitored. If you have any questions or need help, please email us at roadtestnotifications@gmail.com or use the contact page.
